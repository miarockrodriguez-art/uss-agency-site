Miami Security Coverage

U.S.S. Agency serves businesses, residential communities, events, and executives across our full service footprint with licensed armed and unarmed officers, executive protection teams, HOA patrols, fire watch, mobile patrol, and specialty coverage. Clients in Miami and the surrounding metro engage us for the same reasons clients everywhere else do: vetted officers, real field leadership, documented proposals, and the operational discipline that makes coverage reliable shift after shift.

Every engagement begins with a property walk and threat assessment. A U.S.S. Agency field lead visits your site, reviews the exposure profile and operating environment, and returns a written proposal with recommended officer posture, shift structure, coverage schedule, and total investment. Deployment typically follows within 72 hours of signed agreement.

The U.S.S. Agency Standard

Every officer we deploy is licensed under Florida Statute Chapter 493, Section 6301 et seq. Every armed officer holds a current Class G Statewide Firearms License. Every unarmed officer holds a current Class D Security Officer License. Field leadership includes certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors and personnel with active-duty military experience. No paper officers. No gaps in coverage. No corners cut.

Every Officer. Every Shift. Every Site.

Our training is led by certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors and field leadership with active-duty military backgrounds. We reinforce baseline training continually with de-escalation, legal use-of-force, incident documentation, emergency response protocols, and client-specific site procedures. Most security companies stop at the state minimum. We start there. Every deployment comes with GPS check-in and check-out, digital Daily Activity Reports with photo evidence, real-time incident alerts, and a client-facing dashboard.

When something happens on your property, the person handling it is a local field lead who can be on-site in minutes. Our account managers answer their phones, their emails, and their texts — because that is the job.

Licensing, Insurance, and Compliance

U.S.S. Agency operates under Florida Statute Chapter 493, Section 6301 et seq. — the Florida Private Security Services Act — with our Class B Agency License on file with the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services, Division of Licensing. We carry commercial general liability coverage well above the statutory minimum. Certificates of insurance are available to every client on request and are updated annually. Every officer and every armed officer carries their individual Class D and Class G license on person while on duty, verifiable through FDACS at any time.
