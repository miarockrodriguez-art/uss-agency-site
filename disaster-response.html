Security When It Matters Most

Natural disasters create security vacuums that criminals exploit with devastating efficiency. When hurricanes, floods, tornadoes, and severe storms strike, properties are damaged, alarm systems fail, residents evacuate, and law enforcement resources are stretched to their absolute limits. Looting, burglary, trespassing, and opportunistic crime surge in the aftermath of every major disaster. U.S.S. Agency provides rapid-deployment armed and unarmed security teams that protect properties, businesses, and communities during and after disaster events throughout Florida.

As a licensed security agency in Florida, U.S.S. Agency maintains disaster response protocols and a roster of officers prepared for emergency deployment on short notice. We understand that Florida faces hurricane threats annually and have built our operations to respond decisively when disaster strikes, providing security coverage when our clients need it most and traditional protection systems have failed.

Pre-Storm and Evacuation Security

The security response to a disaster begins before the event arrives. U.S.S. Agency deploys officers to client properties before mandatory evacuations take effect, ensuring that buildings are secured, vulnerable access points are reinforced, and a security presence is established before residents and employees leave the area. Our pre-storm officers verify that properties are properly boarded and secured, document the condition of the property before the storm, and prepare for the period of reduced law enforcement coverage that accompanies major weather events.

Post-Disaster Property Protection

The days and weeks following a disaster are the highest-risk period for looting and property crime. Damaged buildings with broken windows, compromised doors, and exposed interiors attract thieves and vandals who take advantage of overwhelmed police resources and displaced property owners. U.S.S. Agency officers provide 24/7 property protection during the recovery period, preventing unauthorized access, deterring looting, and documenting property conditions for insurance claims. Our guards maintain a visible, armed presence that tells would-be looters the property is protected.

Community and HOA Disaster Security

Residential communities and HOAs are particularly vulnerable during disaster events when gates are damaged, access control systems are offline, and residents have evacuated. U.S.S. Agency provides community-wide security deployments that patrol neighborhoods, manage damaged entry points, verify returning residents, and prevent unauthorized individuals from entering the community under the guise of contractors or relief workers. We coordinate with community association managers and board members to provide security updates and incident reports throughout the recovery period.
