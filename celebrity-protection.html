Elite Protection for High-Profile Individuals

Celebrities, entertainers, professional athletes, corporate executives, and public figures face unique security threats that require protection far beyond what standard security services provide. U.S.S. Agency delivers elite close protection services through armed officers who are trained in executive and celebrity protection methodologies, advance security planning, threat assessment, and discreet personal security operations. As a licensed security agency in Florida, we serve high-profile clients visiting Orlando, Miami, Tampa, and destinations throughout the state as well as nationwide assignments.

Our close protection officers are selected for their experience, physical fitness, tactical proficiency, and ability to operate in high-visibility environments where media, fans, and public attention create complex security challenges. Every officer assigned to celebrity protection understands that discretion is as important as capability, and that their role is to enable the client's lifestyle and schedule while eliminating security risks.

Advance Security and Venue Coordination

Effective celebrity protection begins long before the client arrives. U.S.S. Agency advance teams assess venues, hotels, restaurants, event spaces, and travel routes to identify threats, plan secure movements, and coordinate with local law enforcement and venue security. Our advance work includes identifying emergency exits, establishing communication protocols, planning primary and alternate routes, and verifying that all venues meet our security requirements before the client enters the location.

For high-profile events such as concerts, awards ceremonies, press tours, and public appearances, our advance planning ensures that security operations are invisible to the audience while providing comprehensive protection for the client at every moment.

Travel Security and Motorcade Operations

High-profile individuals are most vulnerable during travel and transitions between locations. U.S.S. Agency provides secure transportation coordination, driver-protective agent teams, and motorcade operations for clients who require seamless security coverage from departure to arrival. Our officers manage airport arrivals and departures, hotel lobby movements, and venue transitions with efficiency and discretion that keeps the client on schedule while maintaining a secure perimeter.

Residential and Lodging Security

Whether a client is staying at a hotel, renting a private residence, or visiting a personal home, U.S.S. Agency provides residential security details that control access, patrol the perimeter, monitor for surveillance or stalking activity, and ensure the client's privacy and safety throughout their stay. Our officers operate around the clock in shifts that maintain constant coverage without compromising the client's privacy or comfort.
