Elite Protection for High-Profile Individuals

Celebrities, entertainers, professional athletes, corporate executives, and public figures face unique security threats that require protection far beyond what standard security services provide. Agency delivers elite close protection services through armed officers who are trained in executive and celebrity protection methodologies, advance security planning, threat assessment, and discreet personal security operations. As a licensed security agency in Florida, we serve high-profile clients visiting Orlando, Miami, Tampa, and destinations throughout the state as well as nationwide assignments.

Our close protection officers are selected for their experience, physical fitness, tactical proficiency, and ability to operate in high-visibility environments where media, fans, and public attention create complex security challenges. Advance Security and Venue Coordination

Effective celebrity protection begins long before the client arrives. U.S.S. Agency advance teams assess venues, hotels, restaurants, event spaces, and travel routes to identify threats, plan secure movements, and coordinate with local law enforcement and venue security. Our advance work includes identifying emergency exits, establishing communication protocols, planning primary and alternate routes, and verifying that all venues meet our security requirements before the client enters the location.

For high-profile events such as concerts, awards ceremonies, press tours, and public appearances, our advance planning ensures that security operations are invisible to the audience while providing comprehensive protection for the client at every moment.

Travel Security and Motorcade Operations

High-profile individuals are most vulnerable during travel and transitions between locations. U.S.S. Agency provides secure transportation coordination, driver-protective agent teams, and motorcade operations for clients who require seamless security coverage from departure to arrival. Our officers manage airport arrivals and departures, hotel lobby movements, and venue transitions with efficiency and discretion that keeps the client on schedule while maintaining a secure perimeter.

Residential and Lodging Security

Whether a client is staying at a hotel, renting a private residence, or visiting a personal home, U.S.S. Agency provides residential security details that control access, patrol the perimeter, monitor for surveillance or stalking activity, and ensure the client's privacy and safety throughout their stay. Our officers operate around the clock in shifts that maintain constant coverage without compromising the client's privacy or comfort.

Our Protection Process

Every celebrity protection engagement begins with a confidential threat assessment. Our team evaluates the client's public profile, known threats, upcoming schedule, travel plans, and personal preferences to develop a protection plan that addresses identified risks while accommodating the client's lifestyle and professional obligations. This assessment is conducted with complete discretion, and all documentation is secured and access-restricted.

Once the protection plan is established, we assemble a team matched to the assignment's requirements. For short-term engagements such as a single public appearance, we deploy a focused team with advance coordination. For extended assignments such as a multi-city tour or long-term residential protection, we build a rotating detail with consistent personnel who develop familiarity with the client's routines and preferences. Throughout every engagement, our operations center maintains communication with the detail and is prepared to coordinate rapid response if conditions change.

Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I arrange celebrity protection? We recommend engaging our team at least two weeks prior to an event or assignment to allow adequate time for advance security planning and venue coordination. However, U.S.S. Agency maintains rapid-deployment capability for urgent protection needs and can assemble a team on short notice when circumstances require it.

Can your officers work alongside venue security and law enforcement? Yes. Our close protection officers are experienced in coordinating with venue security teams, local law enforcement, and event management staff. Seamless interagency coordination is a core component of effective celebrity protection, and our officers are trained to operate within these collaborative frameworks.

Do you provide protection outside of Florida? U.S.S. Agency provides celebrity protection services nationwide. Our team regularly deploys across multiple states for tours, productions, and events. We handle all licensing and coordination requirements for out-of-state assignments.

What level of confidentiality do you maintain? Absolute confidentiality is a non-negotiable standard at U.S.S. Agency. Every officer and staff member involved in celebrity protection operates under binding non-disclosure agreements. We do not disclose client identities, assignment details, or operational information under any circumstances without explicit client authorization.

Can you provide both armed and unarmed officers? Yes. We deploy armed or unarmed close protection officers based on the client's preference, the threat assessment, and the legal requirements of the assignment location. Many clients prefer a blended approach with armed officers in certain environments and unarmed officers in others.
