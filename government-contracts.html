Professional Security for Government Facilities

Government buildings, municipal facilities, and public institutions require security services that meet stringent regulatory, compliance, and operational standards. U.S.S. Agency provides armed and unarmed security guards for government contracts at the federal, state, and local levels throughout Florida. As a licensed security agency in Florida, we maintain the licensing, insurance, bonding, and operational documentation required to fulfill government security contracts with full compliance and accountability.

Our officers deployed on government contracts undergo enhanced vetting procedures including comprehensive background investigations, drug screening, and verification of all required certifications. We understand that government security assignments demand personnel who meet the highest standards of professionalism, reliability, and conduct, and we deliver officers who consistently exceed those expectations.

Facility Access Control and Screening

Government facilities require controlled access environments where every individual entering the building is identified, verified, and authorized. U.S.S. Agency officers manage lobby security, credential verification, visitor registration, and package screening at government buildings. Our guards enforce facility-specific access policies, manage contractor and vendor entry, and maintain accurate logs that satisfy government audit requirements.

For facilities with elevated security classifications, our officers implement multi-layer access control procedures that include identification verification, escort protocols for visitors, and restricted area monitoring. We coordinate with facility security managers to ensure our procedures align perfectly with agency-specific security directives and Standard Operating Procedures.

Compliance and Documentation

Government security contracts carry strict reporting and compliance requirements that exceed those of private sector engagements. U.S.S. Agency maintains comprehensive documentation including officer training records, incident reports, daily activity logs, and performance metrics that meet or exceed the requirements of government contracting officers. Our administrative team supports contract compliance with timely reporting, personnel file maintenance, and audit-ready documentation at all times.

Municipal and Public Safety Support

Local governments and municipalities contract U.S.S. Agency for security services at courthouses, public parks, transit facilities, water treatment plants, and community centers. Our officers support public safety operations without carrying law enforcement authority, providing a professional security presence that complements existing police and sheriff resources. We work within the chain of command established by the contracting agency and maintain clear communication protocols with local law enforcement.
