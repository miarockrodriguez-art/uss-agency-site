Professional Security for the Corporate Environment

Corporate offices and business campuses require security programs that protect employees, visitors, intellectual property, and physical assets while maintaining the professional image that corporate environments demand. U.S.S. Agency provides armed and unarmed security officers for corporate facilities throughout Orlando and Central Florida, delivering security services that integrate seamlessly into professional business operations.

As a licensed security agency in Florida, U.S.S. Agency understands that corporate security extends beyond physical protection. Our officers contribute to a secure work environment that supports employee productivity, protects sensitive information, and demonstrates to clients, partners, and stakeholders that your organization takes security seriously at every level.

Lobby and Reception Security

The corporate lobby is both the public face of your organization and its primary access control point. U.S.S. Agency officers staff reception desks, manage visitor sign-in procedures, verify credentials, and control access to executive floors and restricted areas. Our guards maintain a professional, well-groomed appearance that aligns with the corporate environment while providing the security awareness necessary to identify unauthorized visitors, suspicious packages, and potential threats before they enter the workspace.

Executive and Employee Protection

Corporate executives and employees may face targeted threats from disgruntled former employees, business disputes, stalking situations, or activist groups. U.S.S. Agency provides security assessments that identify threats to specific individuals and implements protective measures including secure parking escorts, executive floor access control, and visitor screening protocols designed to prevent unauthorized contact with targeted employees. For elevated threat situations, our armed officers provide close protection services that ensure executive safety during business hours and events.

Campus and Multi-Building Security

Corporate campuses with multiple buildings, parking structures, and outdoor spaces require coordinated security coverage that protects the entire property footprint. U.S.S. Agency deploys vehicle and foot patrols across campus properties, manages access at multiple entry points, and maintains communication between officers stationed at different buildings to ensure unified security operations across the entire facility.

Our Corporate Security Process

Every corporate security engagement begins with a comprehensive site assessment conducted by our management team. We evaluate your facility layout, access points, parking infrastructure, current security measures, and specific concerns to develop a security program tailored to your operation. This is not a template approach. Every recommendation is based on the physical characteristics and operational requirements of your specific facility.

Following the assessment, we present a detailed security proposal outlining officer placement, shift schedules, post orders, communication protocols, and escalation procedures. Once approved, we select officers whose experience and temperament match your corporate environment, conduct site-specific orientation, and deploy with full post orders in place from the first shift. Our management team conducts regular quality assurance inspections and maintains open communication with your designated point of contact to ensure ongoing satisfaction. We also provide quarterly security reviews where we analyze incident data, evaluate emerging threats, and recommend adjustments to keep your security program aligned with your evolving operational needs.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the difference between armed and unarmed corporate security? Unarmed officers are appropriate for most corporate environments where the primary role involves access control, visitor management, and professional presence. Armed officers are recommended for facilities facing elevated threat levels, executive protection requirements, or locations in higher-risk areas. We assess your specific needs and recommend the appropriate level of protection.

Can U.S.S. Agency provide temporary corporate security? Yes. We provide temporary security coverage for corporate events, office relocations, employee termination situations, and periods of elevated concern. Our rapid deployment capability allows us to place qualified officers within 24 to 48 hours of request.

How does U.S.S. Agency handle officer scheduling and replacements? We maintain a bench of trained, site-familiar officers for every corporate account. When a scheduled officer is unavailable, a qualified replacement who has been briefed on your facility and post orders fills the shift. You will never experience a gap in coverage or receive an unprepared substitute.
