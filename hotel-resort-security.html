Hospitality Security That Protects the Guest Experience

Hotels and resorts operate in an environment where guest safety and guest experience must coexist seamlessly. U.S.S. Agency provides armed and unarmed security officers for hotels, resorts, and hospitality properties throughout Orlando and Central Florida. Our officers are trained to maintain a professional, service-oriented security presence that protects guests, staff, and property while preserving the welcoming atmosphere that drives repeat business and positive reviews.

As a licensed security agency in Florida, U.S.S. Agency understands the hospitality industry's unique requirements. Orlando's position as one of the world's top tourist destinations means hotels and resorts face security challenges ranging from guest disputes and trespassing to theft, vehicle break-ins, and emergency response during large-scale events. Our security programs address all of these concerns with trained professionals who understand hospitality-grade service.

Lobby and Common Area Security

Hotel lobbies, pool decks, fitness centers, parking garages, and convention areas require consistent monitoring to deter unauthorized access and criminal activity. U.S.S. Agency officers patrol common areas on scheduled and randomized routes, manage after-hours lobby access, and respond to guest complaints and disturbances. Our guards serve as a visible point of contact for guests who have safety concerns while also identifying and addressing threats that hotel staff may not be equipped to handle.

Guest and Visitor Management

Hotels experience high volumes of foot traffic from guests, visitors, delivery personnel, and vendors. U.S.S. Agency officers support front desk operations by verifying visitor identity, managing after-hours access, and responding to room lockout and noise complaint situations. Our guards are trained to handle intoxicated guests, domestic disturbances, and unauthorized room access with professionalism and in compliance with Florida law.

Parking and Exterior Security

Hotel parking lots and garages are frequent targets for vehicle break-ins, theft from luggage, and opportunistic crime. U.S.S. Agency patrol officers provide visible coverage of parking areas, monitor for suspicious activity, and escort guests to their vehicles during late-night hours. Our security presence in parking areas significantly reduces vehicle-related incidents and the liability exposure that accompanies them.
