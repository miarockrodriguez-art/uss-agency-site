Close Protection Services

Executive protection is the highest tier of personal security, requiring officers who combine tactical expertise with the discretion and professionalism demanded by corporate leaders, public figures, and high-net-worth individuals. U.S.S. Agency provides close protection agents who are trained to operate in close proximity to the principal, maintaining constant situational awareness while blending seamlessly into the principal's professional and personal environment. Our executive protection agents in Orlando and Central Florida come from law enforcement, military, and elite security backgrounds with extensive experience in protective operations.

Every close protection assignment begins with a thorough understanding of the principal's daily routine, travel patterns, professional obligations, and personal preferences. We tailor our protective approach to each client, ensuring that security enhances rather than disrupts their lifestyle. Whether the assignment calls for a single close protection agent or a full protective detail, U.S.S. Agency delivers the caliber of service that high-profile individuals require from a licensed security agency in Florida.

Advance Teams and Threat Assessment

Effective executive protection begins long before the principal arrives at any location. U.S.S. Agency deploys advance teams to survey venues, map routes, identify potential threats, establish secure areas, and coordinate with local law enforcement and venue security prior to the principal's arrival. Our advance work includes checking ingress and egress routes, locating emergency medical facilities, identifying safe rooms, and establishing communication protocols with all parties involved in the protective operation.

Threat assessment is an ongoing process that informs every aspect of our executive protection services. Our team monitors open-source intelligence, social media, and relevant threat indicators to identify potential risks to the principal. We assess the threat environment for each location, event, and travel itinerary, adjusting our protective posture accordingly. This intelligence-driven approach to executive protection ensures that our clients receive armed guard services calibrated to the actual threat level they face.

Secure Transportation

Transportation is one of the most vulnerable phases of any executive protection operation. U.S.S. Agency provides secure transportation services that include route planning, counter-surveillance driving techniques, vehicle security checks, and coordination with advance teams at destination locations. Our protective drivers are trained in evasive driving, emergency vehicle operations, and the specific protocols required to transport high-profile individuals safely through any environment.

We provide armored and non-armored vehicle options depending on the threat assessment and client preferences. All transportation operations include primary and alternate route planning, communication with the operations center, and real-time coordination with the protective detail. For multi-vehicle motorcades and complex travel itineraries, our transportation team manages every logistical detail to ensure safe and efficient movement of the principal.

Residential Security

The principal's residence is where they are most vulnerable and where their family members spend the majority of their time. U.S.S. Agency provides residential security services that include perimeter monitoring, access control, surveillance system management, visitor screening, and around-the-clock security officer presence. Our residential security officers work discreetly within the home environment, maintaining a protective posture that provides security without intruding on the family's daily life.

We conduct residential security assessments that evaluate physical security measures, electronic security systems, landscaping vulnerabilities, neighbor proximity, and emergency response capabilities. Based on our assessment, we recommend and implement security enhancements that harden the residence against potential threats while maintaining the privacy and comfort that the principal and their family expect.
