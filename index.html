Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Why is U.S.S. Agency the top choice for security in Orlando?

+

U.S.S. Agency has built its reputation on professionalism, reliability, and a prevention-first approach. Our officers are fully licensed, insured, and many carry law enforcement or military backgrounds. We tailor every engagement to the client's specific needs — making us the trusted choice for businesses, events, and residential communities across Florida.

What qualifications do your armed officers have?

+

All armed officers hold state certifications through approved commissioned security courses, pass thorough background checks and fingerprinting, complete regular drug testing, and undergo ongoing training in force management and emergency response.

How are security service rates determined?

+

Rates are based on several factors: location, total hours required, armed vs. unarmed officers, complexity of the security needs, patrol vehicle requirements, and whether the engagement is temporary or ongoing. We provide transparent, customized quotes for every client.

What are plainclothes security guards and when are they needed?

+

Plainclothes guards operate in everyday attire for discreet protection. They're ideal for executive protection, corporate events, retail loss prevention, and sensitive business matters where a uniformed presence might be disruptive or undesirable.

Why hire off-duty law enforcement?

+

Off-duty law enforcement officers bring active training, legal authority, and real-world experience. They're especially valuable for high-profile events, political functions, crowd control scenarios, and situations requiring the credibility of law enforcement presence.

Do you serve areas outside of Orlando?

+

Yes — U.S.S. Agency serves over 35 cities across Florida including Miami, Tampa, Jacksonville, Fort Lauderdale, West Palm Beach, and throughout Central and South Florida. Contact us to confirm coverage in your area.
