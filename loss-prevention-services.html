Protecting Your Bottom Line From Theft and Shrinkage

Retail theft, employee dishonesty, and inventory shrinkage cost businesses billions of dollars annually. U.S.S. Agency provides trained loss prevention officers for retail stores, warehouses, distribution centers, and commercial operations throughout Orlando and Central Florida. Our loss prevention specialists are trained to identify theft behaviors, monitor high-risk areas, and implement deterrence strategies that reduce shrinkage and protect your profitability.

As a licensed security agency in Florida, U.S.S. Agency approaches loss prevention as both a security function and a business strategy. Our officers work with your management team to understand your inventory flow, identify vulnerability points, and deploy countermeasures that address both external shoplifting and internal employee theft.

Shoplifting Deterrence and Detection

Professional shoplifters and organized retail crime groups target stores that lack visible security presence. U.S.S. Agency loss prevention officers provide both uniformed and plainclothes deterrence that disrupts shoplifting operations before merchandise leaves the store. Our officers are trained in observation techniques that identify concealment behaviors, tag switching, receipt fraud, and organized theft patterns without creating a hostile environment for legitimate customers.

When theft is detected, our officers follow established apprehension protocols that comply with Florida law and protect the retailer from liability. Every incident is documented with detailed reports, witness statements, and evidence that supports prosecution and civil recovery efforts.

Internal Theft and Employee Dishonesty

Employee theft is responsible for a significant portion of retail shrinkage and is often more difficult to detect than external shoplifting. U.S.S. Agency loss prevention officers monitor point-of-sale transactions, receiving processes, inventory handling, and employee behaviors that indicate potential dishonesty. Our officers work discreetly within the store environment to identify patterns of internal theft without disrupting operations or creating distrust among honest employees.

Inventory Audits and Vulnerability Assessments

Effective loss prevention begins with understanding where your vulnerabilities are. U.S.S. Agency provides security assessments that evaluate your store layout, inventory management procedures, employee access controls, and surveillance coverage to identify gaps that thieves exploit. Based on these assessments, we recommend and implement loss prevention strategies tailored to your specific business environment and inventory profile.
