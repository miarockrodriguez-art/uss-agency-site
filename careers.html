Why Work at U.S.S. Agency

U.S.S. Agency is not a typical security company. We do not operate like the large national firms that treat officers as interchangeable commodities, deploying undertrained personnel to fill posts at the lowest possible cost. We operate as a professional security organization where every officer is a trained specialist — recruited, vetted, and developed to perform at a level that our clients expect and that the industry should demand but rarely delivers.

When you join U.S.S. Agency, you join an organization that values professionalism, accountability, and career growth. Our officers work with a dedicated operations team that provides clear post orders, responsive supervision, and consistent support throughout every assignment. We believe that when officers are treated as professionals and given the tools and leadership they need, the quality of service they deliver reflects that investment. Our clients see the difference, our officers feel the difference, and our reputation confirms it.

We are looking for individuals who take pride in their work, who understand that security is a serious responsibility, and who are committed to conducting themselves with discipline and integrity on every post, every shift, and every assignment. If that describes you, U.S.S. Agency is the right place to build your career in security.

Armed Security Officer

Licensed Class G armed security officers for high-risk assignments including corporate facilities, construction sites, healthcare campuses, banking institutions, and executive protection details. Armed officers must demonstrate proficiency with their duty weapon and maintain current qualifications throughout their tenure. Assignments are available across Orlando, Central Florida, and statewide.

Requirements: Valid Florida Class D Security Officer License, valid Florida Class G Statewide Firearm License, clean criminal background, pre-employment drug screening, reliable transportation, professional appearance and demeanor. Law enforcement or military experience strongly preferred.

Unarmed Security Officer

Professional unarmed security officers for commercial properties, residential communities, retail plazas, event venues, parking facilities, and corporate lobbies. Unarmed officers provide a visible uniformed presence, conduct patrols, monitor access points, document incidents, and serve as the first point of contact for visitors and tenants. These positions require individuals who can communicate clearly, remain alert throughout their shift, and respond calmly under pressure.

Requirements: Valid Florida Class D Security Officer License, clean criminal background, pre-employment drug screening, reliable transportation, strong communication skills, professional appearance.

Field Supervisor

Experienced security professionals to oversee officer performance, conduct post inspections, respond to field incidents, and serve as the operational link between our officers and the operations center. Field supervisors are responsible for maintaining service quality across multiple posts and accounts, coaching officers on post orders and procedures, and escalating issues to management when appropriate. This role requires leadership experience and a track record of reliability in the security industry.

Requirements: Minimum 3 years security experience, valid Florida Class D License (Class G preferred), supervisory experience, clean driving record, ability to manage multiple posts across a geographic area, strong written and verbal communication skills.

Executive Protection Agent

Highly trained close protection professionals for executive protection assignments including corporate executive details, celebrity and VIP protection, family office security, travel security, and high-net-worth residential coverage. Executive protection agents must be capable of conducting advance work, threat assessments, secure transportation, and close-in protective operations. This is a specialized position requiring a combination of tactical training, professional presentation, and the judgment to operate effectively in high-profile environments.

General Requirements

All candidates for security positions at U.S.S. Agency must meet the following minimum requirements before being considered for any assignment:

  • Valid Florida Class D Security Officer License (unarmed positions) — required by Florida Statute Chapter 493
  • Valid Florida Class G Statewide Firearm License (armed positions) — with current annual qualification
  • Clean criminal background — verified through Florida Level 2 fingerprinting (FDLE and FBI)
  • Pre-employment drug screening — with zero tolerance policy maintained throughout employment
  • Reliable personal transportation — required for commuting to assigned post locations
  • Professional appearance and grooming standards — uniform compliance at all times while on duty
  • Ability to work assigned shifts including nights, weekends, and holidays as required by contract
  • Strong verbal and written communication skills — for client interaction and incident documentation
  • U.S. work authorization — E-Verify compliant

How to Apply

Interested candidates should send their resume and a brief cover letter to careers@ussacademy.org or call 321-304-2430 to speak with our hiring team directly. Please include your current license type (Class D, Class G, or both), your availability, and the geographic area where you are seeking assignments.

All applicants will be contacted within 5 business days of submission. Qualified candidates will be invited for an in-person interview and orientation. U.S.S. Agency conducts background checks, drug screening, and license verification on all candidates before assignment.
