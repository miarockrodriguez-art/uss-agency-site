Armed Security for Financial Institutions

Banks and financial institutions are high-value targets that demand the highest caliber of armed security protection. U.S.S. Agency provides state-certified armed security guards for banks, credit unions, and financial service offices throughout Orlando and Central Florida. Every armed officer assigned to a financial institution holds a valid Florida Class G Statewide Firearms License and has completed specialized training in financial facility security protocols, robbery response procedures, and threat identification specific to the banking environment.

As a licensed security agency in Florida, U.S.S. Agency understands that bank security serves dual purposes: deterring criminal activity through visible armed presence and protecting employees and customers in the event of a security incident. Our officers project authority and readiness while maintaining the professional, customer-friendly demeanor that financial institutions require.

Robbery Deterrence and Response

The presence of an armed security officer inside a bank branch is one of the most effective deterrents against robbery. Criminals conducting pre-robbery surveillance look for vulnerable targets, and branches with visible armed security are significantly less likely to be targeted. U.S.S. Agency officers are trained in robbery response protocols that prioritize the safety of employees and customers above all else. Our guards understand their role during and after a robbery event, including witness management, evidence preservation, and law enforcement coordination.

Lobby Security and Customer Monitoring

Bank lobbies require constant observation for suspicious behavior, loitering, and pre-robbery surveillance activity. U.S.S. Agency officers are trained to identify behavioral indicators that suggest criminal intent, including unusual photography of the interior, repeated visits without conducting transactions, and nervous or concealment behaviors. Our guards maintain a welcoming presence for legitimate customers while staying alert to potential threats that bank staff may be too busy to notice.

ATM and Drive-Through Security

ATM locations and drive-through banking lanes present additional security challenges due to their exposure and the cash transactions they facilitate. U.S.S. Agency provides security coverage for exterior banking areas including ATM vestibules, night deposit areas, and drive-through lanes. Our officers monitor these areas for skimming devices, loitering, and robbery attempts targeting customers during cash withdrawals.
