State-Certified Armed Security Officers

U.S.S. Agency provides highly trained armed security guards who carry the authority, discipline, and tactical readiness required for the most demanding security assignments. Every armed officer on our roster holds a valid Florida Class G Statewide Firearms License, issued by the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services. This means each guard has completed rigorous firearms training, passed state-mandated background checks, and demonstrated proficiency in the safe handling and use of firearms while on duty.

Our armed security guards are not simply personnel with a firearm. They are professionals who understand threat assessment, situational awareness, de-escalation techniques, and the legal framework governing the use of force in the State of Florida. As a licensed security agency in Florida, U.S.S. Agency maintains strict internal standards that exceed state minimums, ensuring our clients receive armed guard services that reflect the highest level of professionalism in the industry.
