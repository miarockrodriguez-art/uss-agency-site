After-Hours Property Response Without the Hassle

Business owners and property managers are frequently called to their properties in the middle of the night to respond to alarm activations, water leaks, power outages, and other emergencies. U.S.S. Agency key holder services eliminate that burden by providing a licensed, bonded, and insured security officer who holds your property keys and responds on your behalf whenever an after-hours situation arises. As a licensed security agency in Florida, we provide this service for commercial properties, retail locations, offices, and industrial facilities throughout Orlando and Central Florida.

Our key holder officers are trained to assess situations, make informed decisions about emergency service calls, secure the property, and provide you with detailed reports so you can address issues during normal business hours rather than at two in the morning. This service is designed to give property owners and managers the peace of mind to sleep through the night knowing a trained professional is handling after-hours responses.

Alarm Response and Property Assessment

When your alarm monitoring company calls with an activation, our key holder officer responds to your property, assesses the exterior for signs of intrusion, enters the building with the keys entrusted to us, conducts an interior walkthrough, identifies the source of the alarm, resets the system, and secures the property. Every response is documented with a detailed report that includes arrival time, findings, actions taken, and any follow-up recommendations.

Our key holder officers also determine whether police, fire, or emergency maintenance services need to be called and coordinate those responses on your behalf. This means you receive a complete report of what happened and what was done about it, without having to leave your home.

Emergency Vendor Coordination

After-hours emergencies often require immediate coordination with plumbers, electricians, HVAC technicians, or restoration companies. U.S.S. Agency key holder officers can meet emergency vendors at your property, provide building access, monitor the work, secure the property after the vendor departs, and report all activity to you. This eliminates the need for you to personally supervise emergency repairs during nights, weekends, and holidays.

Secure Key Management

Entrusting property keys to a third party requires absolute confidence in that organization's integrity and procedures. U.S.S. Agency maintains strict key management protocols with documented chain of custody, secure key storage, and access logs that record every time your keys are accessed. All key holder officers undergo comprehensive background checks, and our bonding and insurance coverage provides financial protection for our clients.
