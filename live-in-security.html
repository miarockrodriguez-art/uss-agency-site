Continuous On-Premises Protection

Live-in security represents the highest level of residential protection available. Unlike shift-based security where officers rotate on and off the property, live-in security officers reside at the client's estate or property, providing an uninterrupted security presence that eliminates coverage gaps entirely. U.S.S. Agency provides live-in armed and unarmed security professionals for private estates, luxury residences, and high-net-worth individuals throughout Florida.

As a licensed security agency in Florida, U.S.S. Agency selects live-in security officers through an elevated vetting process that goes beyond standard background checks. Live-in officers must demonstrate exceptional judgment, discretion, professionalism, and interpersonal skills because they operate within the client's personal living environment. Every officer we assign to a live-in post is carefully matched to the client's specific requirements, household dynamics, and security needs.

Estate Security and Property Management Support

Large estates with multiple buildings, extensive grounds, and numerous access points require security officers who know every inch of the property. Live-in security officers from U.S.S. Agency develop intimate knowledge of the estate's layout, routines, vulnerabilities, and personnel. This familiarity allows them to detect anomalies that a rotating shift officer might miss, whether it is an unfamiliar vehicle on a service road, a gate left unsecured, or a change in a neighbor's behavior pattern.

Beyond traditional security duties, our live-in officers often support property management functions such as accepting deliveries, coordinating service provider access, monitoring alarm systems, and managing gate operations. This dual-role capability makes live-in security an efficient solution for clients who want comprehensive property oversight from a trusted professional.

Personal and Family Protection

For high-net-worth individuals and families, live-in security provides a level of personal protection that extends beyond the property perimeter. Our officers are trained to manage daily security logistics including vehicle inspections, route planning, visitor screening, and emergency evacuation procedures for all family members. The constant presence of a trained security professional provides peace of mind that cannot be replicated by cameras, alarms, or part-time security guards.

Discretion and Confidentiality

Live-in security officers operate within the most private spaces of a client's life. U.S.S. Agency enforces strict confidentiality agreements with all live-in personnel and maintains rigorous standards for discretion, professional boundaries, and client privacy. Our officers understand that their role is to protect without intruding, maintaining a professional presence that integrates into the household without disrupting the client's lifestyle or family dynamic.
