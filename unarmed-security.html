Professional Unarmed Security Officers

U.S.S. Agency provides highly trained unarmed security guards who deliver dependable protection without the escalation risks associated with armed personnel. Every unarmed security officer on our team holds a valid Florida Class D Security License, issued by the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services. This license requires completion of a state-approved 40-hour training course covering legal authority, emergency procedures, observation and reporting, and professional conduct standards for security officers in the State of Florida.

Benefits of Unarmed Security Guards

Unarmed security guards offer a range of advantages that make them the ideal choice for many commercial, residential, and institutional environments. The presence of a professional unarmed officer communicates authority and accountability without creating an atmosphere of tension or intimidation. For businesses that prioritize customer experience alongside safety, unarmed security provides the perfect balance.

  • Non-threatening presence: Unarmed guards project authority while maintaining an approachable, professional demeanor that puts employees, customers, and visitors at ease. This is particularly important in environments where public interaction is frequent, such as retail stores, office buildings, and residential communities.
  • Cost-effective protection: Unarmed security services typically cost less than armed guard deployments, allowing businesses to maintain continuous security coverage within their budget. This makes 24/7 protection accessible for small and mid-sized businesses that might otherwise forego security services entirely.
  • Community-friendly approach: In residential communities, apartment complexes, and HOA-managed properties, unarmed guards build positive relationships with residents. They serve as a familiar, trusted presence that enhances the sense of community while deterring unwanted activity and enforcing property rules.
  • Reduced liability exposure: Unarmed security deployments carry lower insurance and liability considerations compared to armed assignments, making them a practical choice for property owners and managers who want professional security without the added complexity of firearms on site.
  • Versatile deployment: Unarmed officers can be deployed in virtually any setting, from corporate lobbies and hospital entrances to parking structures and outdoor events. Their training in access control, customer service, and incident reporting makes them adaptable to a wide variety of security roles.

Qualifications and Training Standards

Every unarmed security officer deployed by U.S.S. Agency meets or exceeds the following qualification standards:

  • Florida Class D Security License: All officers hold a valid D License issued by the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services, confirming completion of the required 40-hour training program
  • Comprehensive background check: Every officer passes a thorough criminal background screening at both the state and national level before being assigned to any post
  • Drug screening: All officers complete pre-employment drug testing and are subject to random screenings throughout their employment
  • Ongoing professional training: Officers participate in regular training updates covering emergency response, de-escalation techniques, report writing, customer service, and site-specific procedures
  • Physical fitness standards: Officers maintain the physical condition necessary to perform patrols, respond to emergencies, and stand post for extended shifts
  • Professional appearance and conduct: All officers report to duty in proper uniform with valid credentials and are expected to maintain the highest standards of professionalism at all times

U.S.S. Agency invests in the continuous development of our officers because we understand that the quality of our personnel directly reflects the quality of protection our clients receive. We do not cut corners on training, and we do not deploy officers who have not met every requirement in our qualification process.
