Vetting and licensing

Every officer passes a multi-stage background check, drug screen, and credential verification before they are placed on a client site. Armed officers complete annual firearms requalification. Every license is kept current and on person. If an officer's license lapses, they are off the roster the same day — no exceptions.

Training led by real instructors

Our training is led by certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors and seasoned field leadership with active-duty military backgrounds. We reinforce baseline training continually with de-escalation, legal use-of-force training, incident documentation, emergency response protocols, and client-specific site procedures. Most security companies stop at the state minimum. We start there.

Technology you can actually see

Local field operations

Our Licensing and Compliance

U.S.S. Agency operates under Florida Statute Chapter 493, Section 6301 et seq. — the Florida Private Security Services Act — with our Class B Agency License on file with the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services, Division of Licensing. We carry commercial general liability coverage above the statutory minimum. Certificates of insurance are available to every client on request and are updated annually. Every officer and every armed officer carries their individual Class D and Class G license on person while on duty, and every license is verifiable through FDACS at any time.
