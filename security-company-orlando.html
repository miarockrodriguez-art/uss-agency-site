Protection Without Compromise

The difference between a security company you hire and a security company you trust comes down to one question: who is actually on the post when something happens? U.S.S. Agency was built around the answer. Every officer on our roster is individually vetted, licensed, trained to standard, and accountable to a field lead who knows their name. We serve businesses, residential communities, executives, events, and sensitive environments. Our clients include corporate headquarters, multi-family apartment complexes, homeowners associations, private schools, hospitals, places of worship, construction sites, retail plazas, and hospitality properties. Every engagement begins the same way: a field lead reviews your exposure profile and returns a written scope of work and a proposal you can compare against any other offer in the market. The U.S.S. Agency Standard

Every officer we deploy is licensed, vetted, and trained to a standard measurably higher than the state minimum. Armed officers hold an active Class G Statewide Firearms License. Unarmed officers hold an active Class D Security Officer License. Field leadership includes certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors and personnel with active-duty military experience. No simulations. No corners cut. No second-rate officers in our uniform.

Elite Licensed and Insured Security Services

U.S.S. Agency is a fully licensed, bonded, and insured security provider operating under Florida Statute Chapter 493, Section 6301 et seq. — the Florida Private Security Services Act. Our corporate agency license is on file with the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services, Division of Licensing. We carry commercial general liability coverage well above state minimums. Certificates of insurance are available to every client on request and are updated annually. Every individual officer carries their Class D and Class G license on person while on duty, verifiable through FDACS at any time.

Every Officer. Every Shift. Every Site.

Our training is led by certified Law Enforcement Firearms Instructors and seasoned field leadership with active-duty military backgrounds. We reinforce baseline training continually with de-escalation, legal use-of-force training, incident documentation, emergency response protocols, and client-specific site procedures. Most security companies stop at the state minimum. We start there. Every deployment comes with real technology clients can actually see — GPS check-in and check-out, digital Daily Activity Reports with photo evidence, real-time incident alerts to the client and account manager, and a client-facing dashboard.

When something happens on your property, the person handling it is local. You don't get routed to a regional call center. You get a field lead who can be on-site in the time it takes to make a coffee. Our account managers answer their phones, their emails, and their texts — because that is the job.

Our Process

Every engagement begins with a property and threat assessment delivered by a U.S.S. Agency field lead. We evaluate your site layout, access points, exposure profile, operating environment, and any existing security procedures. We review what you already have in place and where the real gaps are. Based on that assessment we develop a customized security plan that specifies officer posture, shift structure, coverage schedule, post orders, reporting formats, and communication protocols. Officers are assigned to the account, briefed, and deployed — and our management team stays engaged with ongoing performance audits, reporting reviews, and adjustments as your needs evolve.

Frequently Asked Questions

Are your officers licensed and insured? Yes. Every officer we deploy holds a current Florida Class D Security Officer License, and every armed officer also holds a current Class G Statewide Firearms License. U.S.S. Agency operates under its Class B Agency License issued by the Florida Department of Agriculture and Consumer Services. We carry commercial general liability coverage above statutory minimums and provide certificates of insurance on request.

Can you provide armed or unarmed officers? Yes. We provide both, and we help clients determine which posture is appropriate based on the threat profile, setting, and operating environment. Many clients deploy a mix — armed officers at high-risk posts and unarmed officers for access control and concierge positions.

Do you offer 24/7 coverage? Yes. U.S.S. Agency phones and field operations are answered 24 hours a day, 7 days a week. We deploy single-shift, multi-shift, 24/7, and event-based coverage profiles depending on client need.

How fast can you deploy? Most engagements see officers on site within 72 hours of signed agreement. Emergency deployments — fire watch, workplace violence response, short-notice executive protection — can often be staffed within 24 hours.

Do you write custom proposals? Every proposal is custom. We do not send cookie-cutter scopes. A field lead visits your site (in person or virtually depending on scope), documents your exposure profile, and returns a written proposal with recommended officer posture, shift structure, coverage schedule, and total monthly investment.
